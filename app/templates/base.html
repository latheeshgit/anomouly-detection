How It Works

  1. Select one or multiple files (up to 1GB in total) to upload to the cloud.
  2. Start the transfer process with a single click.
  3. Our system monitors for suspicious activities during the transfer.
  4. If no anomalies are detected, your files are securely uploaded to Google Drive.
  5. If an anomaly is detected, the transfer is halted, and you're alerted immediately.
  6. View comprehensive logs of all transfer activities in the Results page.
